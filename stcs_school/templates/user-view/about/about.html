Welcome to Taralabalu Central School established since 2012

SRI TARALABALU CENTRAL SCHOOL - AJJIHALLI was established in 2012. We are located in outskirts of Channagri block of DAVANAGERE district of Karnataka. Our school consists of Grades from 1 to 10. Our school is a Co-educational. English is the medium of instructions in our school. Our school is approachable by all weather road. In our school academic session starts in mid of May.

Our school has Private building. It has got 25 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. We have 3 other rooms for non-teaching activities and also has a separate room for Head master. The source of Drinking Water in our school is Well and it is functional. Our school has 5 boys toilet and 5 girls toilet and it is functional. Our school has a playground. Our school has a library and has 550 books in it. We haves 45 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. We don't provide mid-day meal.
